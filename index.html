Welcome to Loch Quarry Outdoor Centre

Nestled in the heart of breathtaking natural landscapes, Loch Quarry Outdoor Centre offers the perfect setting for adventure, learning, and connection with nature. Whether you're a seasoned explorer or just beginning your outdoor journey, our activities are designed to inspire, challenge, and entertain all ages.

At Loch Quarry, we believe in the power of the outdoors to ignite curiosity and foster unforgettable experiences. From discovering local wildlife to mastering new skills, our range of activities allows you to explore the world around you while connecting with family, friends, or fellow adventurers. Each visit is an opportunity to grow, bond, and create lasting memories.

With a commitment to sustainability and education, Loch Quarry Outdoor Centre is more than a place to play—it’s a gateway to appreciating and protecting the natural world. Our team of passionate instructors is dedicated to providing safe, inclusive, and engaging experiences for everyone, ensuring that every guest leaves with a deeper connection to nature and a sense of achievement.
